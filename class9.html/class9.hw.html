The Fabumouse Mozart



|

It was a cold rainy January night.
Lucky for me I was warm and cozy inside my mouse hole.I was nestled in my favourite pawchair infront of a cheery fire.
"This is the life!" I squeaked popping a chocolate cheese cupcake into my mouth and opening my book.I felt so relaxed.Everything was so peaceful. But then............
Rattle!Rattle
The window was rattling right behind my chair!
I decided to play some some soothing music.
