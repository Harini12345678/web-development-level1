My Top 3 Favourite Movie Series

Zombies


I love the move Zombies. It has altogether 3 movies:Zombies;Zombies2;Zombies3. All movies show case different creatures coming together.In the first movies humans and zombies come together. In the second movie werewolves join, and in the third one aliens join them.



Harry Potter


I'd rank Harry Potter as my third favourite movie series. It has 8 movies. It all starts with 11 year old Harry Potter who lives with his not-so nice aunt and uncle and cousin. He gets accepted at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.




Descendents


Descendents is my second favourite movie series.It has 3 parts:Descendents,Descendents2 and Descendents3. It starts with evil Villian Kids(VKs) who are trapped on the Isle of the Lost.The kids get a chance to go to Auradon Prep a schoolfor the Aks(Auradon kids). The series ends with the barrier preventing the villians from getting out is taken down.
